# Clarity

**Clarity** is a browser extension designed to create a healthier social media experience by filtering NSFW content, thirst traps, ragebait posts, and ads. It helps users focus on meaningful content instead of endless, sexualized, clickbait, or promotional feeds.

---

## Problem

Social media platforms like Instagram, Facebook, YouTube, and TikTok often promote:

- Sexualized content  
- Thirst traps and suggestive posts  
- Emotionally manipulative (ragebait) content  
- Intrusive ads and sponsored posts  

Users have limited control over what appears in their feeds, which can negatively affect focus, productivity, and overall digital well-being.

---

## Solution

Clarity is an **AI-assisted browser extension** that improves social media browsing by:

- Detecting and blurring NSFW images and suggestive posts  
- Identifying and hiding ragebait or clickbait posts  
- Removing or hiding ads and sponsored content  
- Cleaning feeds by hiding addictive sections like Instagram Reels or YouTube Shorts  
- Optionally replacing blocked content with neutral or helpful messages  

Clarity focuses on **feed-level control**, rather than blocking entire websites.

---

## Core Features (MVP)

- **NSFW Filter** – Blur or hide explicit or sexualized images and posts  
- **Thirst Trap Detection** – Filter suggestive content commonly found on social media  
- **Ragebait Detection** – Identify emotionally manipulative posts and hide them  
- **Feed Manipulation** – Hide or replace distracting posts  
- **Focus Mode** – Remove addictive feed sections like YouTube Shorts or Instagram Reels  
- **Ad Blocking** – Hide sponsored posts, banners, and promotional content

## Development Roadmap

### Phase 1 – MVP (Current)
- Keyword-based filtering for NSFW, thirst traps, and ragebait posts  
- Basic ad blocking  
- Focus mode: hide Shorts/Reels  

### Phase 2 – AI Integration
- Image classification for NSFW and suggestive content  
- Automatic detection of ragebait posts using text analysis and sentiment scoring  
- Feed manipulation with replacement cards for blocked content  

### Phase 3 – Advanced Features
- User customization: whitelist/blacklist, toggle individual filters  
- Statistics dashboard: posts blocked, time saved, focus score  
- Notifications and reminders for productivity  
- Theme/visual customization: dark/light mode, blur intensity  
- Cross-platform synchronization for user settings
